Get started with Drycc Workflow in three simple steps:

1. **Install CLI tools** for Helm and Drycc Workflow
2. **Boot a Kubernetes cluster and install Drycc Workflow**
3. **Deploy your first application**

This guide helps you set up a cluster suitable for evaluation, development, and testing. When you are ready for staging and production, view our [production checklist](../managing-workflow/production-deployments.md).

## Step 1: Install Workflow

[Install Drycc Workflow](install-workflow.md) on your infrastructure.

## Step 2: Install CLI tools

[Install the Drycc Workflow CLI tools](install-cli-tools.md) on your local machine.

## Step 3: Deploy your first app

Finally, [log in and deploy your first application](deploy-an-app.md).
